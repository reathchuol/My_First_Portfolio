The ICE - DevelopA Project

The ICE - DevelopA (Innovation Centre for Education Development in Africa) project is purposed towards providing equitable digital education system in the rural areas of Sub-Saharan Africa. It involves developing an adaptive learning application that enables seamless online and offline education for both learners and teachers. The platform is designed to automate key teaching processes while maintaining an intuitive, user-friendly interface.
